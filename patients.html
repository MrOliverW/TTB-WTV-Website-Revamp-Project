What is CareCloud?

CareCloud offers patients personalized and secure online access to portions of their medical records. It allows you to safely use the internet to manage and receive important health information.

With CareCloud, you can:

  • Request medical appointments
  • View your health summary
  • Access test results
  • Request prescription renewals
  • Communicate electronically and securely with your care team

Is there a fee?

CareCloud is a free service offered to our patients.

Have Questions About CareCloud? We’re Here to Help.

Whom do I contact if I have further questions?
If you have technical questions about using CareCloud, please contact our support team at patient-first@ntdimaging.com or call NTDI Patient Support at 972-681-4000.

For questions about your medical records or care, please reach out directly to your provider’s office. You can also use CareCloud’s secure messaging feature for non-urgent communication with your care team.
