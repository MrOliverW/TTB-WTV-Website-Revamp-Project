Our Story

North Texas Digital Imaging (NTDI) has proudly served Forney and the surrounding communities since 2007. Originally established as Forney Diagnostic Imaging, the practice relocated to Mesquite in 2010 to better accommodate our growing network of physicians and patients. Today, we continue to expand our reach while staying committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centered radiological services.

Our experienced team of compassionate professionals is dedicated to providing advanced diagnostic imaging using state-of-the-art technology. We are proud to have a staff of Board Certified radiologists and nationally registered technologists, many of whom hold advanced credentials in multiple specialties. Each member of our technical staff has received extensive training in both equipment operation and imaging protocols to ensure accuracy, safety, and comfort with every procedure.
